Calvin Klein

Calvin Klein is an iconic American fashion house renowned for its minimalist aesthetic, provocative advertising campaigns, and a wide range of products including apparel, underwear, fragrances, and home furnishings.

Dior

Christian Dior is a prestigious French luxury fashion brand celebrated for its haute couture, ready-to-wear collections, leather goods, footwear, jewelry, fashion accessories, fragrances, and cosmetics that embody timeless elegance and innovative design.

Dolce & Gabbana

Dolce & Gabbana is an Italian luxury fashion house known for its glamorous, sensual, and often opulent designs that draw inspiration from Sicilian culture and Italian sartorial traditions.

Gucci

Gucci is a high-end Italian fashion brand, part of the Kering Group, distinguished by its innovative and progressive designs, often featuring bold patterns, signature motifs, and a blend of traditional craftsmanship with contemporary flair.

Guess

Guess is a global lifestyle brand recognized for its denim, apparel, and accessories, which are characterized by their youthful, adventurous, and distinctly American-inspired style.

Louis Vuitton

Louis Vuitton is a globally recognized French luxury fashion house, specializing in leather goods, handbags, trunks, shoes, watches, jewelry, and accessories, famously known for its distinctive monogram pattern and heritage of craftsmanship.
